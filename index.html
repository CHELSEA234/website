Xiao Guo

I am a final-year Ph.D. student in the CVLab of Michigan State University, advised by Prof. Xiaoming Liu. Before that, I spent years as a research programmer at USC/ISI, advised by Prof. Iacopo Masi and Prof. Wael AbdAlmageed. I obtain my master and bachelor at the University of Southern California and Wuhan University of Technology, respectively.

My research interests include:

  • Generative AI: Multi-modal Language Model (M2F2-Det; MM-Det; DDVQA-BLIP) and Image Generative Model (DenseFace).
  • Responsible AI: Image Forgery Detection (HiFi-Net; HiFi-Net++) and Face Anti-Spoofing (FAS-Wrapper).

I have been a main contributor to several major U.S. government-sponsored projects, including MediFor, ODIN, and RED. In addition, I spent two wonderful summer as an intern at Amazon in 2023 and 2024.
